How to Play

Welcome to the game! Here’s how you can play:

  • Goal: Capture as many stones as possible by distributing them in the right order.
  • Players: Two players take turns to play.
  • Start: Enter Player 1 and Player 2 names to begin.
  • Taking Turns: On your turn, click on a box with stones to distribute them.
  • Distributing Stones: Distribute stones in the boxes following a counter-clockwise direction.
  • Capture Stones: If a box has exactly 4 stones after distributing, it is captured and your score increases by 4 points.
  • End of Turn: Once all stones are distributed, your turn ends, and the other player starts.
  • Winning: The player with the most stones captured at the end of the game wins!At the end interesting Gameplay is also there.
